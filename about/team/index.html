Design Careers Club

Design Career Fair is brought to you by the efforts of a group of talented, hardworking and passionate students-- better known as Design Careers Club at UC Davis. We volunteer our time in bringing career-building events, opportunities and resources to Design students here at UC Davis. The majority of the leg work is done by specific teams that all take care of important tasks for reaching club goals, while officers have the responsibility of seeing our club's aspirations come true! Get to know our teams and click on names for a link to a member's portfolio!

Officers

Directors organize, manage and lead team officers to pursue the goals of the club, for members and the design community at UC Davis. They all delegate tasks for team members to complete to assure the club achieves its goals. They encourage club members’ efforts and support each other through thick and thin.

Outreach

The outreach team are the first to contact employers and aim to build long-term relationships in the future. Reaching out to design faculty and Alumni of Davis, the outreach team invites professionals to participate in events like Alumni Networking Day and the Design Career Fair.

Graphics

The graphics team exercises their visual design and motion graphics abilities, designing promotional posters, banners, flyers and motion graphics videos that give the club a strong sense of brand identity.

Web

The web team are a lively bunch of coders who map out the club on a digital plane. They are hard at work reshaping the DCC website and developing the DCF 2017 website for employers, students and the rest of the design community to enjoy.

Event Experience

The event experience team plans, organizes, structures and masters all club led events. This unstoppable team leaves nothing to chance and instead powers over events with great precision. Their job is to gather volunteers and ensure that all the necessary equipment, food and services are ready to go on the day of an event.

Exhibits

The exhibits team creates expressive and dynamic exhibition designs for the 3 main events of the year. They produce exciting and interactive displays that provide a fun environment for attendees to explore. The exhibits team strengthens the memorability of events as well as enhances the overall theme by pushing beyond 2 dimensional boundaries.

Finance

Finance is in charge of keeping team projects within budget. They quantify exactly how much we can contribute and provide at each event given our financial standing. They brainstorm ways to raise funds through fundraisers and create club socials to enhance morale.

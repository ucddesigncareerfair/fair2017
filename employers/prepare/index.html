Prepare

Congratulations! You've decided to attend the 2017 UC Davis Design Career Fair! We know you'll have a blast and meet many wonderful UCD Designers. To make the most of your visit, we've compiled some useful information below:

Before the Fair

  1. Register for the fair

    "If you haven't already, reserve your spot by shooting us an email at designcareersclub@gmail.com. We'll need your registration as soon as possible to properly accommodate and market your company's attendance!"

  2. Decide what to bring

      We suggest you bring...

    • A company banner
    • Brochures or informational flyers regarding open company positions
    • A sign-up sheet for students interested in company updates
    • Materials to foster engagement (wearable buttons, activities, etc)
    • Fun freebies
    • A folder to collect resumes, etc.
    • Light sweaters or jackets for brisk Davis mornings and A/C indoors
    • Laptop chargers, for those bringing computers

      We can and will provide...

    • Breakfast when you arrive
    • Lunch at noon
    • 8’ x 2.5’ tables with table skirts
    • 2-4 chairs
    • Outlets for your electronics
    • Customized table place cards
  3. Get excited!

    Seriously, we're looking forward to hosting you. We hope you're just as excited to meet our amazing students and colleagues!

Day of the Fair

  1. Get to the fair

      Driving on Interstate 80, (from West or East):

    • Look for signs for California 113 North towards Woodland.
    • Take ramp onto CA 113 North.
    • After merging with 113 North, keep right.
    • Take Exit 27 towards UC Davis.
    • Merge onto Hutchinson Dr.
    • Turn left onto La Rue Rd.
    • Turn right onto Orchard Rd.
    • Orchard Rd turns into Parking Lot 25, park here!

    You can also find us on Google Maps

  2. Park at the ARC

    An all-day parking permit can be purchased from the yellow kiosks at the entrance to Lot 25 for $9.

  3. Come on in!

    The building at the West end of Lot 25 is the Activities and Recreation Center (ARC). The northern entrances facing Lot 25, pictured below, leads to the ballrooms - where you'll find the UC Davis Design Career Fair! Welcome!

We look forward to having you!
